Title: Math Painting
Description: An app that let's the user provide the start coordinates of geometroical shapes such as squares and
rectangles, their dimensions, and their colors, and the program produces an image files canvas with the geometrical
shapes drawn on it.
Object: Square:
            x
            y
            side
            color
            draw(canvas)
        Rectangle:
            x
            y
            width
            height
            color
            draw(canvas)
        Canvas:
            width
            height
            color
            make(imagepath)
